Zombies
Attack

Our first game made on Unity!

During one of my Computer Vision courses, I had the opportunity with a friend to make a game project in 3D on Unity. As big fans of games as CS GO and Valorant from Riot Games, we decided to create a first-person shooter. The game consists in waves of zombies spawning from the cemetery and trying to attack the house of the player. The player needs to protect his house and to do so, he can kill the zombies to earn money. With the money gained, he can buy turrets to help him kill the zombies or he can block some of the ways out to his house.

This project was really fun to make, my friend did a big part of the coding while I helped him more on the practical parts of the game as well as the design. After such a project we plan to make other games that we can publish!
